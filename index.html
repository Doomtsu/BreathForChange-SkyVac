SkyVac: Revolutionary Air Cleaning Technology

Our Mission

Breath For Change is pioneering the future of atmospheric cleaning with SkyVac - our innovative fleet of AI-piloted kites and balloons. Our technology captures soot, dust, and CO₂ directly from the troposphere, transforming pollution into valuable products while powering sustainable communities.

How SkyVac Works

Our self-powered SkyVac platforms use flexible solar panels and advanced onboard energy systems. Each unit employs ultra-light electrostatic grids to attract and capture particles, with AI navigation ensuring maximum air-cleaning efficiency while maintaining safe flight patterns.

SkyVac Technology Breakthrough

⚡

Ultra-Light Electrostatic Grids

Flexible, lightweight meshes that attract and capture tiny particles like soot, dust, and CO₂ using minimal energy.

AI-Powered Navigation

Advanced algorithms steer SkyVac units to chase pollution hotspots in real time, maximizing air-cleaning efficiency.

Self-Powered Platforms
